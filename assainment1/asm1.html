SURETRUST

Skill Upgradation for Rural-
youth Empowerment

Why SURE Trust ? There is an urgent need to make quality training in skills upgradation more accessible to rural youth considering the fact that half of India’s population is expected to be in rural India by 2050 and the rural work force accounting for 70% of the total workforce.

Vision of SURE Initiative Enhancing the employability of educated unemployed rural youth through ‘Human values-based skill up-gradation in emerging technologies, with no cost to students.
Purely a service-oriented & a non-profit initiative The initiative is based on the dual principle of 'Giving Back to the Society' and 'Service to Youth is Service to Nation'.
What does SURE INITIATIVE do ? Makes effort to bridge the gap between the skills acquired from academic institutions and the skills required by the industr
A unique intellectual & sustainable “grama-seva” initiative Quality training for skill upgradation is paramount in a technology dominated world. Rural youth miss out of the initiatives due to remoteness. Hence there is an urgent need to bring the educated unemployed rural youth into the main stream. This online training is available across india.
Initiated and managed by SSSIHL alumni and Sai Devotees The SURE Trust is initiated and managed by the alumni of Sri Sathya Sai Institute of Higher Learning, Pransanthi Nilayam, Puttaparthi, Anantapur District, Andhra Pradesh, and the devotees of Bhagawan Sri Satya Sai Baba.

Uniqueness Of Our Training

Bridging gap between the skills acquired through academic degrees and the skills required in the industry
Mandatory training in Education in Human Values / Life Skills
The cost students have to pay for each course is 'discipline, regularity and Punctuality but not money.
